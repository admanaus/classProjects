ABOUT OUR STORE

We are your one-of-a-kind toy shop for buying, selling, and trading LEGO® products. LEGO® fans and traders for all of Utah County; from The point of the mountain at the north of Utah Valley all the way down South to Payson and Santaquin, and everything in between (including Eagle Mountain).

Huge Selection

Bricks & Minifigs buys, sells, and trades all things LEGO®. You will not find a larger selection of LEGO® Minifigures® and bricks in all of Utah County! We have everything in our 2,500 square foot store in Orem - from rare and retired sets and pieces to overflowing bins of bricks you can sort through and buy in bulk! We also have a large selection of LEGO® accessories, collections, and even displays of unique builds and creations. We have an ever-growing inventory of Minifigures®, as well as a wide variety of both standard and unique bricks in a multitude of colors. We have collectible minifigs as well as build-your-own minifigs. Come in and reimagine and rebuild your lego sets and creations through what you find in our vast collection of bulk and minifig pieces. You can also find pieces to complete your LEGO® sets and update your collection! Our inventory changes all the time, so stop in and let us help you find the right LEGO® parts or sets. Visiting our store can be a different and fun adventure every time! While you’re here, ask about our current competitions, workshops, and giveaways. We also have gift cards for those special occasions and birthdays.

Themed Birthday Parties & Corporate Events

We have a whole party room set aside for the most engaging and captivating parties. Our birthday party packages are mountains of fun for boys and girls alike! Call us to schedule a LEGO® themed birthday party (or other event). We even have a LEGO®-Car derby track, as well as LEGO® Zipline racing! People love our parties!
